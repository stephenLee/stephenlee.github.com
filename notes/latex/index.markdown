LaTeX
========

## Command for argmax and argmin ##

```
\DeclareMathOperator*{\argmax}{arg\,max}  % in your preamble
\DeclareMathOperator*{\argmin}{arg\,min}  % in your preamble 

\argmax_{...} % in your formula
\argmin_{...} % in your formula 
```
